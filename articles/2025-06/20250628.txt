星期六                                                     第九週
Saturday                                                  Week Nine

「我的內心不平靜」慧可說道。「請平撫我的心靈。」
「把你的心帶到我身前」波地哈瑪回答,「我將會平撫它。」
「但當我設法尋找我的心時」慧可說,「我無法發現它」。
「你瞧!」波地哈瑪手指一彈,「我已平撫你的心靈。」

——Wumen kuan

"I have no peace of mind," said Hui-k'o.
"Please pacify my mind."
"Bring out your mind here before me, "replied
Bodhidharma, "and I will pacify it!"
""But when I seek my own mind," said Hui-k'o
"I cannot find it."
"There!" snapped Bodhidharma. "I have pacified
your mind."

——Wumen kuan
