星期二                                                     第九週
Tuesday                                                  Week Nine

廬山煙雨浙江潮,
未到千般恨不消。
到得原來無別事,
廬山煙雨浙江潮。

———宋朝,蘇東坡

Misty rain on Mount Lu, the River Che at high tide
Before you have been there, longing to go eats at you endlessly.
When you have been there and come back,
Just misty rain on Mount Lu, the River Che at high tide.

——Su Tung-Po, Sung Dynasty, China
