星期日                                                        第七週
Sunday                                                    Week Seven

神聖的顯現就在我們前方，
左邊是火，右邊是美麗的溪流。
無論誰走在火中，
都會突然出現在冰冷的溪流中。
任何藏於水面下的源頭，
都自火中探出。

大部分人保衛自己免於走進火中，
而最後卻掉進其中……
如果你是上帝的朋友，
火就是你的水。
你應祈求你有成千上萬對飛蛾的翅膀，
你便可燒掉它們，
一晚一對。

——Rumi

The divine presence is there in front of us,
fire on the left,
a lovely stream on the right…
Whoever walks into the fire
appears suddenly in the cool stream,
Any head that goes under the surface of the water
that head pokes out of the fire.

Most people guard against going into the fire,
and end up in it….
If you are a friend of God,
fire is your water.
You should wish to have a hundred thousand
sets of moth wings,
So you could burn them away, one set a night.

——Rumi
