Wednesday                                                Week Nine

自我是控制的座位,它識別自主行為,而身體的許多功能(循環、消化、生長)都是非自主的。
為什麼我們只將一小部分自我識別為「你」?

The ego is the seat of control... It identifies voluntary behavior,
while many of the body's functions (circulation, digestion, growth)
are involuntary. Why do we identify only a small portion of the self as "you"?
——Ken Wilber

自我感到特別受困於身體對疼痛的脆弱性,它試圖麻痺身體以減少痛苦。
這種麻痺身體的代價是失去快樂。

The ego feels especially trapped by the body's vulnerability to pain...
It tries to numb the body to reduce suffering. The price of numbing the body is the loss of joy.
——Aurobindo

接受非自主行為作為自我的一部分,可以放棄對創造的控制,從而獲得更大的自由。
整個有機體在沒有「自我」幫助下協調數百萬個過程的智慧。

Accepting involuntary behavior as part of the self allows us to give up control over creation,
thus gaining greater freedom. The wisdom of the entire organism coordinating millions of processes
without the help of the "ego."
——Ken Wilber

我們應該將生命建立在更廣闊的自然智慧和自由之上,而不是自我引以為傲的膚淺技巧。

We should build our lives on the broader natural wisdom and freedom,
not on the superficial skills that the ego is proud of.
——Ken Wilber
