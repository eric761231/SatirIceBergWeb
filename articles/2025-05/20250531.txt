「恆常」無需特別的努力。
做得一樣不變的能力去接受事物原有的樣貌。
對那些不知「空」為何的人來說，這能力會顯現是耐性，
但耐性其實是可接受的，也能夠拒於。
單純直覺而知道「空」的人，
總是開放可能，接受善事物原有的樣貌，
他們能欣賞所有事物。

——Shyunryu Suzuki Roshi
  鈴木俊隆
  ZenMind, Beginner's Mind


…in constancy there is no particular effort involved--there is only the unchanging ability to accept things as they are. 
For people who have no idea of emptiness, 
this ability may appear to be patience, 
but patience can actually be non-acceptance. 
People who know, if only intuitively, 
the state of emptiness always have open the possibility of accepting things as they are. 
They can appreciate everything.

——Shyunryu Suzuki Roshi
  ZenMind, Beginner's Mind
