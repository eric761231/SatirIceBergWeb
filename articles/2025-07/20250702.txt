思緒一個接一個,從不間斷。
但如果你允許這些思緒串結成鍊,你將作繭自縛。

——傳統禪說

One thought follows another
without interruption. But if you
allow these thoughts to link up
into a chain, you put yourself in
bondage.

——Traditional Zen saying
