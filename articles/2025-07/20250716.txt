星期三                                                     第十二週
Wednesday                                                Week Twelve

芭蕉葉上無愁雨
只是時人聽斷腸

——Zenrinkushu
  禪林句集

The raindrops patter on the basho leaf
But these are not tears of grief
This is only the anguish of the one
Who is listening to them

——Zenrinkushu
