星期五                                                     第十週
Friday                                                    Week Ten

趙周問：「什麼是道？」
老師回答：「你的平常心就是道。」
「我怎樣才能以平常心回到合一呢？」
「一旦有了合一的意圖，你立刻就脫軌了。」
「但若是缺乏意圖，我如何能瞭解道？」
「道不屬於知，也不屬於不知」老師說
「知道是錯誤的瞭解，不知是盲目的忽視。
如果你真能超脫、毫無懷疑地瞭解道，
那就像萬里晴空，又為何要牽連到是與非呢？」

——Wu-men kuan

Chao-chou asked, "What is the Tao?"
The master replied, "Your ordinary mind is the Tao."
"How can I return to oneness with my ordinary mind?"
"By intending to be at one you are immediately off course."
"But without intention, how can I know the Tao?"
"The Tao," said the master, "belongs neither to knowing nor
not knowing. Knowing is false understanding; not knowing
is blind ignorance. If you really understand the Tao beyond
doubt, it's like the empty sky. Why drag in right and wrong?"

——Wu-men kuan
