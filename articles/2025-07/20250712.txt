星期六                                                     第十一週
Saturday                                                  Week Eleven

以必勝之心踏上戰場,
必能全身而退。
作戰時抱著破釜沈舟之必死心態,
你定能活著回來。
存有一絲求存之希望便必死無疑。
離家時決心不再見面,
你便會安全而返。
一丁點回家的盼望已能帶你踏上不歸路。

——Uesugi Kenshin
  上杉謙信

Go to the battlefield firmly confident of victory
and you will come home with no wounds whatever.
Engage in combat fully determined to die and
you will be alive. Wish to survive in battle and
you will surely meet death. When you leave the
house determined not to see it again, you will come
home safely. When you have any thought of
returning you will not return.

——Uesugi Kenshin
