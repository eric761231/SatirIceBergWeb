星期一                                                   第二十一週
Monday                                                  Week Twenty One

……一切有感覺的生命都是靈，除此之外並沒有別的存在。
這靈沒有初始，它是未生的、也是不滅的。
它不是綠的也不是黃的，沒有形式也沒有外貌，
它不屬於任何存在或不存在的物類，
無法想成新或舊，沒有所謂的長或短、大或小，
因為它超越了一切限制、計量、名稱、形跡與比較，只是醒覺於一靈。

——Huang Po
  黃檗

…all sentient beings are nothing but one spirit, beside which nothing exists. 
This spirit, which is without beginning, is unborn and indestructible.
It is not green or yellow, and has neither form nor appearance. 
It does not belong to the categories of things which exist or do not exist, nor can it be thought of in terms of new or old. 
It is neither long nor short, big nor small, for it transcends all limits, measures, names, traces, and comparisons. Only awake to one spirit.

——Huang Po
