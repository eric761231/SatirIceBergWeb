星期六                                                     第二十二週
Saturday                                                  Week Twenty Two

人們總認為外在的情況會擾亂心靈，現象會阻礙清晰的理解力，所以
他們總是想逃離外在的情境以使心靈平靜，拋開現象以保護他們的理
解力。他們並不了解，其實是心靈擾亂了外在的情況。因此，若你讓
心靈淨空，外在的情況自然也會淨空；若你能讓清晰的理解力平靜下
來，現象自然也會平靜下來。不要以相反的方式運用你的心靈。

——Huang Po
黃檗

People always say that outside states obstruct the mind,
that phenomena obstruct clear understanding. So they
always want to escape from outside states to make their
minds peaceful, and to renounce phenomena to protect
their understanding. They do not realize that it is the mind
which obstructs outside states. Therefore, if you cause the
mind to be empty, outside states will be naturally empty
as well, and if you cause clear understanding to be calm,
phenomena will be naturally calm as well. Do not use the
mind in an upside-down way.

——Huang Po
